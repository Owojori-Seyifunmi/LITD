About Us

Light In The Darkness (LITD C.I.C.)

Light In The Darkness is a Community Interest Company dedicated to bringing light, hope, and support to society's most vulnerable individuals. We exist to ensure that vulnerability is met with structure, stability, and a clear pathway toward independence.

Based in Greater Manchester, we work with marginalized families and individuals who are struggling to access the help they need. Through tailored, person-centered interventions, we empower those facing hardship by providing practical, emotional, and advocacy support.

We are also partnered with a psychologist who provides specialized support for both children and adults.

Why Our Work Matters

Bridging the Gap When Systems Fail

In an ideal system, statutory services would function as intended, ensuring that all individuals receive the support they need before reaching a crisis point. However, the reality is starkly different. Many families and individuals only access these services when they are already in extreme distress.

Since our establishment in 2017, we have witnessed firsthand how vulnerable people are often treated unfairly—not only due to a lack of resources but also because of systemic biases and institutional prejudices within healthcare, social services, education, and the criminal justice system.

LITD exists to bridge this gap, advocate for fairness, and ensure dignity is upheld.

When accountability is resisted, advocacy becomes essential. We stand alongside our beneficiaries to ensure they are treated fairly.

The Reality on the Ground

Children unable to attend school due to unresolved incidents and communication breakdowns

Parents and children experiencing suicidal thoughts due to neglect and lack of intervention

Families living in hazardous housing conditions that put them at grave risk

Young people subjected to daily hate crimes, yet their experiences are dismissed

Individuals facing mental health crises, stuck in bureaucratic systems

Our Support Model

Strength-Based Recovery

The Need

LITD C.I.C. primarily works with vulnerable individuals from Global Majority communities who are experiencing poor mental and physical health due to trauma, systemic oppression, and poverty.

Too often, society defines these individuals by their struggles rather than recognizing their strengths. At LITD, we take a strength-based approach to recovery, acknowledging the potential, skills, and resilience of those we support.

Our Purpose

Our project aims to aid recovery by helping individuals reconnect with their skills, rebuild their confidence, and move forward in life.

We recognize that this journey is not easy, as they continue to face barriers such as poverty and financial hardship, systemic oppression and racism, and mental and physical health challenges.

Intended Impact

  • Reducing social isolation and fostering belonging
  • Restoring a sense of purpose
  • Supporting recovery and raising aspirations
  • Developing employment skills
  • Enhancing soft skills
  • Celebrating culture and diversity
  • Advocating for fairness

Our Founder

Beatrice Guessie

Founder & Director | Light in the Darkness (LITD CIC)
Director | The True Anchor Housing Ltd

Beatrice Guessie is a safeguarding-focused leader committed to strengthening pathways toward stability and independence for vulnerable individuals. She is the Founder and Director of Light in the Darkness (LITD CIC), a community organisation delivering structured support for young people and displaced adults — including refugees and asylum seekers — through early intervention, mentoring, sport, and community integration.

In parallel, Beatrice leads The True Anchor Housing Ltd, reinforcing her commitment to developing safe and sustainable housing pathways that promote long-term stability and successful independent living.

Her work reflects a strategic vision centred on prevention, partnership, and resilient communities, with the ambition of building trusted services that respond to both immediate needs and long-term life transitions.
