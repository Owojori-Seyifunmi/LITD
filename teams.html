YL

Yvette Ledjon

Director

Qualified Senior Social Worker registered with Social Work England. She primarily works with vulnerable adults, from young adults (18+) to elderly individuals in later life. Her expertise lies in supporting individuals with care and support needs, particularly those at risk of neglect or abuse.

Driven by a deep commitment to social justice, Yvette is dedicated to helping people navigate their environments effectively. She advocates for independence, connects individuals and families to essential resources, and promotes overall well-being. Additionally, she plays a crucial role in safeguarding basic care needs and ensuring the collective well-being of individuals and families. Yvette is committed to improving lives, alleviating concerns, and empowering individuals to achieve social justice.

  • Educates families on overcoming barriers within Social Services
  • Provides tailored advice and information through direct support and group sessions
  • Empowers beneficiaries by helping them understand their rights and responsibilities
