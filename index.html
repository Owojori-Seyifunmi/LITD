About Us

Light In The Darkness (LITD C.I.C.)

LITD CIC delivers safeguarding-led, high-performance programmes that empower young people and women through holistic, trauma-informed support and structured pathways into resilience, wellbeing, and positive adulthood outcomes. Working strategically alongside statutory partners and community stakeholders, we design and implement targeted interventions that improve safety, strengthen families, and deliver measurable, long-term community impact.

Why Our Work Matters

Bridging Gaps Through Strategic Partnership

In an ideal system, individuals would receive timely, preventative support before challenges reach crisis point. However, many young people and families currently access services at a later stage, when needs have become more complex.

Since 2017, our frontline experience has provided valuable insight into the varied and sometimes complex pathways across health, education, social care, and justice systems, particularly for vulnerable communities. LITD CIC works collaboratively with statutory partners to strengthen these pathways through early intervention, safeguarding-led support, and holistic programmes that promote resilience, stability, and positive life outcomes.

LITD CIC exists to bridge gaps in provision, champion fairness, and uphold dignity for young people and families facing complex challenges.

We deliver safeguarding-led advocacy and holistic support, empowering individuals to navigate systems, access the right help at the right time, and build resilience through collaborative, solution-focused partnerships.

The Reality on the Ground

Many of the individuals we support face complex and deeply affecting challenges that often go unaddressed, impacting their safe transition from childhood into adulthood and their ongoing recovery as adults.

Children unable to attend school due to unresolved safeguarding issues

Parents, young people, and adults experiencing emotional distress and suicidal thoughts as a result of neglect or trauma

Young people and adults affected by hate crimes, exploitation, or community safety risks without adequate intervention

Individuals of all ages trapped in mental health crises within rigid systems

Adults and young people struggling to rebuild their lives and achieve stability after prolonged adversity

Our Services

Supporting the Disadvantaged With Care, Dignity, and Purpose

As a Community Interest Company, LITD C.I.C. delivers holistic, person-centred support to individuals, families, and young people facing social, emotional, and economic challenges.

Mental Health Support

Accessible mental health and wellbeing support through trusted professionals and community-based initiatives.

  • Partnerships with psychologists
  • Counselling and therapy
  • Mental health workshops
  • PTSD support

Youth Empowerment

We work with young people to prevent disengagement, build confidence, and create positive pathways for the future.

  • Digital and sports initiatives
  • 6-month entrepreneurship programme
  • Volunteering opportunities
  • Leadership skills

Educational Opportunities

Supporting individuals, especially women from marginalised communities, to access education and achieve independence.

  • Recognised qualifications
  • Leadership training
  • Hair wrapping sessions
  • CIC start-up support

Social Prescribing

Non-clinical support that addresses the root causes of poor wellbeing and social exclusion.

  • Grief support
  • Domestic violence pathways
  • Walking groups
  • Befriending programmes

Advocacy and Mentorship

Every individual deserves a voice and a guide. Our advocacy services ensure fair treatment when navigating complex systems.

  • Support navigating education systems
  • Social services advocacy
  • Mentorship programmes
  • Life skills development

Culturally Appropriate Food Bank

Addressing a critical gap in traditional food aid services by ensuring dignity and cultural familiarity.

  • Food support during benefits wait
  • Culturally appropriate items
  • African chef collaboration
  • Nutritional suitability

Our Support Model

The Need

We primarily support individuals from Global Majority communities affected by trauma, systemic oppression, poverty, and poor mental or physical health. Rather than defining people by their struggles, we focus on their strengths, skills, and potential.

Our Purpose

We help individuals reconnect with their abilities, rebuild confidence and self-worth, navigate ongoing barriers such as poverty, racism, and health challenges, and move forward with dignity and hope.
